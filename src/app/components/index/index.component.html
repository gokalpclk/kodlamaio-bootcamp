What is the MILTEK Project?

In order to increase the economic benefit of digitalization, the need for the development of digital economy applications, domestic and national innovative technologies, the development of human resources working in these fields and the transformation of the workforce have arisen in order to support national software. CASGEM aims to support youth employment to meet this need, to contribute to the digital transformation of young people and to help them meet the expectations of the labor market, with the Project of Supporting the Qualified Employment of Youth within the Framework of the National Technology Move (MILTEK) carried out in 2021-2023.

What is the Bootcamp?

Bootcamps are a new education model for those who want to enter a technology career. Companies that want to grow their team organize bootcamps. People who have started to gain technical skills but are not ready to enter a job apply to bootcamps. Selected candidates receive admission to 4-8 weeks of free and intensive training. Students who are successful in bootcamps get a job in the relevant company.
